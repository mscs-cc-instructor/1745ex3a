Meaning of Community

Wasteland is a collective of artists and designers from around the Midwest. With its disruptive, graphic-led approach, the collective takes its cues from post punk, underground comics, skateboarding, and the spirit of subculture as a whole. Wasteland is not one person, nor is it one idea. It sits in the space between people.Projections would include user engagement and shifts in personal perspective. Site development will align with the overall artist interests and challenge users to take abstract concepts (emotional state, mental illness, gender, sexuality, etc.) and allow to it be digested in digital form.The benefit of this interaction would result in the artist’s print media being sold. In order to ensure that every user has the equal accessibility to each artist’s work there will be direct contact information for the artists. Although, this may seem to be an unnecessarily extra step for the user, it creates an inclusive and uplifting platform for individual artists to showcase their social media presence along with personal communication to each user/purchaser. This approach will minimize the capitalistic and competitive nature that exists within then creative community. Wasteland does not function as a profit orientated marketspace.

A deep burning passion that was formed through the many and varied real-life experiences that can only be found by getting out there amongst it. By exploring off the beaten path, by eating things we can’t pronounce, and listening to bands we’ve never heard of. Generally, just living it.

As we see it, without passion we are nothing.

It’s a terrible business plan, truth be told, but at the end of the day it’s yours and it’s ours: we own this thing together. Sometimes we can pay the bills, usually not, but we persevere because it’s who we are.

Besides, what the fuck else are we going to do?

It’s been our goal from day one to simply be an authentic representation of youth culture, whatever and wherever that may be. We are committed to documenting the lifestyles in which we live in the restless and reckless manner that they deserve. In today’s uptight world that stifles so much great creative exploration, What Youth must and will always break free from those limitations and be that true representation of today’s youth culture.

We are you and you are us.

What Youth is much more than just a media voice. What Youth is a living and breathing noisemaker for those looking to wring a little more out of their own life on the run, which may or may not include a surfboard or a skateboard. How you get your kicks is up to you, we’re simply here to document it.

If you’re reading this, thank you for coming along for the ride. We hope we won’t disappoint.
