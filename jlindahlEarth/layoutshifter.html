Shop

When it comes to our daily lives it is pretty difficult to live without going to a grocery or department store. One way you can keep our planet healthy is to shop local, bring your own cloth bags and limit the amount of plastic packaged items. Look for items packaged in biodegradable materials. Plastic fills our landfills and never goes away!
