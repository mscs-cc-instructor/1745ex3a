About Us

We are a group of well trained and experienced musicians who love what we do while being dedicated to providing professional quality musical services to our customers. We've received numerous awards for our performances and are constantly hearing enthusiastic feedback about our services from our many clients that have worked with our team on a friendly and professional basis.

It took just a little encouragment from people within the community to transform what started out as mearly a few friends meeting together on the weekends to share music. It grew to an organization of about a dozen musicans providing music for all sorts of events in the community.

We provide many combinations of instruments and musical styles to service customers and enjoying every moment of it. From the inital contact with a client till the exciting day of the event, we make it our goal to engage with them in the most helpful way so that they receive exactly what they need to give their guests a satisfying experience.
