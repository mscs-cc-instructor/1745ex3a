About Us:

Like all good businesses, Gold Tree Apparel started as a dream in a basement. With a lot of hard work, determination, and a lot of passion, Gold Tree became what you know, and love, today.

We can do both screen printing and embroidery on a lot of different garments. We can even embroider towels and screenprint on totebags! Just contact us to see what we can put together for you.
