Building a PC

The first step towards building a pc is to select what parts you want to build it with. The bare minimum each PC needs in order to function is to have Motherboard, RAM, CPU, CPU cooler, GPU, PSU, case, and a hard drive or ssd. To read about the definitions and functions of each of those go to Definitions.
